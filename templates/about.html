About HFX Dog Friendly

HFX Dog Friendly is a locally owned and operated website to share all of the local restaurants, stores as well as public spaces where you can bring your furry companion.


Want to know what places are dog friendly in the area? Use the search bar to find out.


Know a location that is dog friendly but not listed on the site? Fill out our contact us form to send the information to be added.
